United Catalyst Corporation Joins the ARA Educational Foundation 2014 Catalytic Converter Donation Program, “Ed U Cat”

April 7, 2014 Becky Berube

-(Greenville, SC) April 7, 2014

United Catalyst Corporation is pleased to join with Automotive Recyclers and the ARA Educational Foundation (ARAEF) to "give back" to the Association that tirelessly educates and advocates for the Industry. 

"Giving has never been easier than with Ed U Cat.  Recyclers who are recycling converters through our program, Converter IDentification (CID) Systems™, can tag the converters they wish to donate or specify a dollar amount. We will simply cut a check on their behalf to the ARAEF and send it to them to present." said United Catalyst Corporation president, Becky Berube. "We believe in corporate giving programs that have significant impact and the ARA Educational Foundation certainly does. We hope everyone will join in the Ed U Cat Annual Fundraiser."

Converter IDentification (CID) Systems™ - Data in hand, cash in the drawer

October 29, 2013 Becky Berube

Converter IDentification (CID) Systems™ is helping recyclers get what they want from their scrap catalytic converters – data in hand and cash in the drawer. Know the value of the converter by make/model and VIN upfront before you buy the car or cat. Get paid how you need to – when you need to – without compromising full value.

Converters are not just another core. They are a precious metals commodity. These are the metals that should be sold after processing and refining so that the true end-of-life value of the converter can be revealed.

It takes a leading expert in both processing and refining to manage an assay-based catalytic converter recycling program. United Catalyst Corporation offers recyclers 20 years expertise in both areas. Scientifically accurate and reliable results every time from a leading global refiner, assay-based selling can effectively eliminate grading and pricing issues associated with selling converters by the piece.

United Catalyst Corporation is an independent processor of scrap catalytic converters and converter testing facility. The company is the creator of Converter IDentification (CID) Systems™, a partnership with North American recyclers and converter manufacturers, to process, test, and refine all scrap catalytic converters by make/model and VIN, returning true value for this commodity to the recycler.

Know true converter values. Become a recycling partner with United Catalyst Corporation and reap the benefits of Converter IDentification (CID) Systems™. It’s your commodity, learn to manage it.
